Lasagne

Scariatcho's lasagne

This is a simple economical dish that contains some healthy veg without you noticing it.

Ingredients

  • Lasagne Sheets
  • Mince Meat
  • Tomatoes Chopped
  • Tomatoe Puree
  • Basil
  • Black Pepper
  • Fine Chopped Onions
  • White Roux
  • Cream
  • Parsley, chopped

Method

  1. pour large glass of red wine
  2. sweat onions in a pan over medium heat in a little oil or lard
  3. Add mince,brown mince gently
  4. horse in the tomatoes and puree
  5. cook for a while, have the glass of wine
  6. add basil and pepper, and salt if needed (tomatos are salty already)
  7. add sugar if it tastes sour
  8. make white sauce
  9. Addwine, cream and parsley together and bring to boil
  10. add roux and reduce het, let thicken
  11. Arange Lasange sheets in cooking tray
  12. pour meat mix on top
  13. add another layer of sheets
  14. continue until meat mix is used
  15. Chuck white sauce on final layer of sheets
  16. cook tomorrow, finish wine now
